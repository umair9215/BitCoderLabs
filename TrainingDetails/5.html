NFTP Orientation Ceremony

22 August, 2022

BitcoderLabs, a leading technology company known for its innovative software solutions, recently attended the National Freelancing Training Program's orientation ceremony to show support for the program's efforts to promote freelancing as a viable career option. The orientation ceremony provided an opportunity for program participants to learn more about the program's training and development initiatives, as well as to meet with program managers, trainers, and industry experts. The BitcoderLabs team was impressed by the program's commitment to promoting entrepreneurship and self-employment, and by its emphasis on providing high-quality training and mentorship to aspiring freelancers. During the ceremony, the BitcoderLabs team had the opportunity to speak with program participants, share insights and experiences, and provide guidance on technology and software solutions that could support their work as freelancers. The team emphasized the importance of innovation, creativity, and teamwork in driving success in the technology industry, and encouraged participants to pursue their passions and take risks in pursuit of their goals. The BitcoderLabs team also had the opportunity to meet with program managers and trainers to discuss potential areas of collaboration, including the development of new training modules, mentorship and internship programs, and the provision of technology and software solutions to support the program's operations. Overall, BitcoderLabs' visit to the National Freelancing Training Program's orientation ceremony was a valuable opportunity to show support for the program's efforts to promote entrepreneurship and self-employment, and to explore opportunities for collaboration and partnership in pursuit of this goal. The visit highlighted the importance of collaboration and mentorship in promoting innovation and entrepreneurship, and underscored the value of partnerships between the private sector and government agencies in driving social and economic development.
