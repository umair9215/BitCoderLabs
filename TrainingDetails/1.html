Session on Freelancing Training in Im Sciences Peshawar

22 March, 2022

Bitcoderlabs recently organized a freelancing session at IM Sciences Peshawar to provide students with valuable insights into the world of freelancing. The session was attended by a large number of students and faculty members who were eager to learn more about the opportunities and challenges of working as a freelancer. The session began with an overview of the freelancing industry, highlighting the key benefits of working as a freelancer, such as flexibility, autonomy, and the potential for earning a high income. The session also covered the challenges and pitfalls of freelancing, including the need for self-discipline, time management skills, and the importance of building a strong reputation and portfolio. During the session, students were introduced to popular freelancing platforms such as Upwork, Freelancer, and Fiverr, and were provided with practical tips and guidance on how to create an effective profile, write winning proposals, and land high-paying projects. They were also given insights into the skills and knowledge required to succeed as a freelancer, such as communication skills, marketing skills, and technical skills. The session also featured a Q&A session, where students had the opportunity to ask questions and seek guidance from experienced freelancers. This provided students with a valuable opportunity to learn from the experiences of others and gain insights into the freelancing industry. Overall, the freelancing session organized by Bitcoderlabs at IM Sciences Peshawar was a huge success. It provided students with valuable insights into the world of freelancing, and equipped them with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed as freelancers. The session also provided a valuable opportunity for students to connect with industry professionals and build their professional networks, which can be invaluable in helping them to launch successful freelancing careers.
